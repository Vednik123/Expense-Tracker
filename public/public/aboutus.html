About KhataBook

KhataBook is a simple yet powerful expense tracking application designed to help individuals and small businesses manage their financial transactions efficiently.

Our goal is to provide a hassle-free solution for tracking credits, debits, and maintaining a clear record of all your financial activities.

Key Features

Easy Transaction Entry

Quickly add credits and debits with minimal effort

Transaction History

View and manage all your past transactions

Balance Calculation

Automatic calculation of credits, debits and balance

Dark Mode Interface

Easy on the eyes with modern dark theme
