Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover my forgotten password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a password reset link to your registered email address.

How do I report a transaction error?

You can report transaction errors using the contact form above. Make sure to select "Transaction Problem" from the dropdown and provide details about the error including date, amount, and transaction ID if available.

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Most issues are resolved within 24-48 hours of being reported. Complex issues may take longer. You'll receive regular updates on your registered email address.

Can I use KhataBook on multiple devices?

Yes, KhataBook syncs across all your devices. Simply login with your credentials on any device to access your financial data.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade encryption to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
